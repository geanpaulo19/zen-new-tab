1. Overview

Zen New Tab is a minimal browser extension designed to display time, date, weather, daily quotes, and background images. Your privacy is respected.

2. Data Collection

Zen New Tab does not collect, store, or sell personal data.

3. Location Usage

Location access is used only to retrieve local weather information. This data is processed locally and is not stored or shared.

4. External APIs

The extension may request data from:

  • Open-Meteo (weather data)
  • Nominatim / OpenStreetMap (location name)
  • Quotes API (daily quotes)
  • Image service (background images)

No personally identifiable information is transmitted beyond what is required to fetch these resources.

5. Local Storage

Zen New Tab stores small preferences locally in your browser, such as:

  • 12 / 24-hour clock preference
  • Cached weather and background data (to reduce API calls)

This data never leaves your device.

6. No Tracking

Zen New Tab does not use analytics, cookies, tracking scripts, or advertising services.

7. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated alongside extension updates. Continued use of the extension indicates acceptance of these changes.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns, you may contact the developer at geanpaulo@mail.com.
